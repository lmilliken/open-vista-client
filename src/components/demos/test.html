The Future Earth

The Future Earth Secretariat oversees the day-to-day operations of the programme. Our staff members bring diverse experience and skills in research enabling, coordination, communications, synthesis and foresight, capacity mobilising and more.

The Future Earth Secretariat is currently based in five Global Hubs and in three Regional Centres and Offices. The Global Hubs coordinate the international efforts of Future Earth and are located in Canada (Montreal), France (Paris), Japan (Tokyo), Sweden (Stockholm) and the United States (Colorado).

The Regional Centres adapt and spread the vision of Future Earth in regions around the world. They are based in Asia and the Middle East and North Africa (MENA). One Regional Office has been established in South Asia and a second is in development in South Africa. The Inter-American Institute for Global Change Research in Montevideo, Uruguay, is a strategic partner in Latin America.

You can learn more about the Regional Centres here.

For general inquiries about Future Earth: contact@futureearth.org
